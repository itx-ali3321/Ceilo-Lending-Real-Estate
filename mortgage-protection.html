What Does Mortgage Protection Cover?

Death Benefit

If you pass away, the policy pays off your remaining mortgage balance so your family keeps the home — completely free and clear.

Disability Coverage

If you become disabled and unable to work, monthly benefits cover your mortgage payments so you never miss a payment.

Critical Illness

Diagnoses like cancer, stroke, or heart attack trigger a lump-sum benefit to protect your home during recovery.

Return of Premium

Some plans return all premiums paid if you outlive the policy term — you get protection at absolutely no final cost.

Unlike traditional life insurance that can be used for any purpose, mortgage protection is specifically structured to safeguard your home and provide targeted peace of mind for homeowners and their loved ones — no matter what life brings.
